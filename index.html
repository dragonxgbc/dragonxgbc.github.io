About Me

Hi! My name is Brandon Phuong. I'm a game developer, primarily focused on programming and designing games. I have released a game on ROBLOX; Volleyball 4.2. I'm currently working on a new game project that I hope to release soon in early access. I'm passionate about making fun, accessible experiences for everyone to enjoy. I specifically like building interactive experiences and exploring the intersection of code and design. My work with the concept of memory is exploring the many ideas of how we understand and process memory. A good amount of my coursework features nostalgic themes, with explorations in the malleability of memory. How it forgets, replaces, molds itself around certain events, and affects personality. In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, reading, listening to niche music, and learning about new things. I'm a huge fan of games like Pokémon, The Legend of Zelda, and Monster Hunter. I like listening to music genres like math rock and instrumental scores.
