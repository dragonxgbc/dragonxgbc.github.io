Persona/Archetype Study

Exploring the persona of a jaded young man, focusing on men's mental health and growing apathy for society.

About This Project

This video project delves into the persona of a jaded young man, capturing his internal struggles with mental health and his growing sense of apathy towards society. I wanted to show visual storytelling by using the rain, the dim lighting, and the idea of walls closing in to represent the mental state that many young men face today. The project aims to shed light on the often overlooked emotional experiences of men, highlighting the importance of mental health awareness and the societal pressures that contribute to feelings of isolation and detachment.
